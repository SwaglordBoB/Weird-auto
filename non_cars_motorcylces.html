THE WORLDS FASTEST NON CARS/MOTORCYCLES

The record for the fastest boat is held by Ken Warby, who achieved an incredible speed of 317 kilometers per hour with his homemade vessel.

The fastest unmanned aircraft is the NASA X-43A, reaching Mach 9.6 or about 11,000 kilometers per hour.

The Concorde is one of the most iconic aircraft, achieving supersonic speeds of 2,172 km/h in 1986.
